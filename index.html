About Our Mission

Forward Thinking Innovations is a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) committed to positively shaping the future of American health IT. We lead and inspire those we work with by providing advanced, high-quality, and continuous healthcare solutions.

Our expertise and dedication allow us to partner with businesses, policy makers, and various government agencies to create meaningful, lasting change in how healthcare data is managed and utilized.
